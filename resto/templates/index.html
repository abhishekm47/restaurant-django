About Us

Diwali Palace is regarded as a symbol of royalty and elegance. Here at Diwali Palace, we believe that cooking is a celebration of life and that traditional foods are continuing part of our tradition and culture. At Diwali Palace, we challenge ourselves daily to live up to our name by serving and preparing traditional, Indian and Nepalese home-style meals with a lot of passion, devotion, and grace.

Our menu features a great range of vegetable dishes, non-vegetarian dishes and mouth-watering desserts from India’s rich ethnicity including authentic Nepalese cuisine that tastes as it came straight from Nepal. Every menu item is exclusively tailored with flair and special attention is given to every detail is characteristic of Diwali Palace for every occasion; whether it be a large lunch party, intimate first date, family outing or official dinner.
