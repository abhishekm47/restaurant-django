About Us

Diwali Palace is regarded as a symbol of royalty and elegance. Here at Diwali Palace, we believe that cooking is a celebration of life and that traditional foods are continuing part of our tradition and culture. At Diwali Palace, we challenge ourselves daily to live up to our name by serving and preparing traditional, Indian and Nepalese home-style meals with a lot of passion, devotion, and grace. Each of us has very exceptional memories of home- food, the kind of food that keeps us longing for it when we are away from home. Capturing this authentic aroma has become an essential undertaking for Diwali Palace and we are extremely proud to cook traditional food meals from recipes perfected from over thousands of years.

Diwali Palace food is made by using the premium seasonal ingredients in separate vegetarian as well as non-vegetarian kitchens by our experienced trained chefs. Delicious food has the capability to delight your sense of taste and leave you more contented. Our menu features a great range of vegetable dishes, non-vegetarian dishes and mouth-watering desserts from India’s rich ethnicity including authentic Nepalese cuisine that tastes as it came straight from Nepal. Every menu item is exclusively tailored with flair and special attention is given to every detail is characteristic of Diwali Palace for every occasion; whether it be a large lunch party, intimate first date, family outing or official dinner. Regardless of the occasion, you can all the time expect a beautiful atmosphere with delicious food at the Diwali Palace.
